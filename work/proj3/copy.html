I built a website for the Daily 49er newspaper's first special issue of the Spring 2016 semester: exploring the Long Beach, CA music scene. The issue included band features, venue spotlights, and a live music crawl to four shows around the city. The site is build on a custom WordPress theme with the HTML5 Blank template and the Twitter Bootstrap framework. The site also includes embeded video, responsive images, and an interactive map of historic music landmarks.

The design was meant to showcase the vibrant imagery of performance shots and band portraits. On the cover page, I created an image map of a sound-wave with different segments of the wave linking to a story. Each article in the issue represents a different tone in the collective rhythm of the Long Beach music scene. I wanted the navigation of the site to reflect that unity and amalgam.
