About Myself

Resume available upon request.

Hello, I’m Lindsay Peters. I’m an aspiring front-end developer, designer, and journalist based in Orange County, CA. I have a BA in journalism with a minor in web technologies & applications from Cal State University, Long Beach.

I build clean, responsive websites that communicate information as clearly and straightforward as possible without sacrificing their ability to captivate. While working as a web editor and design assistant for the Daily 49er newspaper, I created dynamic sites for Special Issues using WordPress. However, I also have experience developing static and single-page sites.

My time developing for a daily newspaper tells you I’m used to working long hours under strict deadlines and my slumping posture will tell you I’m pretty much glued to my screen. Still, I love the work I do because it inspires my passion to constantly learn and work through problems. I strive to challenge my habits and practices in order to grow as a developer and improve with each project.

Feel free to Contact Me. I'm open to front-end development positions where I can continue to learn, solve puzzles, and improve my work. All while building sites that have their own story to tell.
